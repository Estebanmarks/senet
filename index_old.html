Standalone one-file version for phone or desktop. You are the black pieces. Tap a highlighted piece on your turn.

Rules in this version

Move all five of your pieces off the board first.
Protected pieces cannot be captured if they are next to a friendly piece or on safe squares.
Square 27 is water and sends a piece back to the nearest free square at or below 15.
You need an exact roll to bear off beyond square 30.
AI uses a built-in heuristic, so this file works fully offline.
